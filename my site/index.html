What is winter sports?

It is attractive for the feeling of running on the snow in an extraordinary silver world. Many people like to skiing or snowboarding on the snow-covered slopes, which gives them a feeling of floating and openness. Fantastic scenery such as the slopes where the sun rises and the night skiing where the snow flutters is worth seeing.
